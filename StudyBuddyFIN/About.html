Our Work

No all students are the same. Some can sit in a class room, listen to the lecturer and walk out fully educated. But others need a bit more help. Even though the university provides extras help like facilitation or demise, there still does not seem to be enough help for the number of students that need it. And thus, Study buddy was born.

This web site application allows student to easy access external help.

There are to types of users namely demi’s and students. If you wish to become a demi’s, all you need is to fill out the electronic form and apply, after conversion we (the team) can except of deny you the opportunity based on your marks. If you want to sign in as a student, you must register for free. When you sign in you will get the option to search for a demi in the subject that you desire. After browsing the profiles, you can choose one and book an appointment at one of our fixed location.
